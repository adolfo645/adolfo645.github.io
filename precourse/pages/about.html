So, my interest on things and how they work is very related to the programming field, as it is what it allows us to use the everyday tools we could not live without. Now that I am in Israel and that ITC has offered such a great opportunity I did not want to let it go by without taking it, as I can now start studying a subject I have always been interested in, and in Israel! With all the implications that means regarding the tech world.
